Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the currency amount for the Blue Card?

    The minimum amount that can be loaded on the card is $100 equivalent amount in BTC and ETH. The maximum amount would be as per PocketBank Card guidelines applicable from time to time. You can learn more about it by here.

  • What steps do I take if my card gets lost?

    The card will be protected by biometric technology. Basically, there is no need to worry about being stolen. You can contact our customer service email:service@pocketbank.tw for the card protection procedure.

  • Do I have to maintain any minimum balance?

    It depends on the card you choose. For example, if you pick an Purple Card, you get a special waiver on the minimum balance requirement.

  • What kinds of tokens does this platform support?

    PocketBank currently supports the storage and payment of mainstream currencies such as BTC, ETH, XRP, LTC, ETC, BCH, DASH, and supports most of their mutual exchanges. To invest in other Defi commodity stablecoins, we recommend using USDT and DAI.

  • Can I make online payments to foreign recipients?

    Yes, you can! PocketBank does not limit cryptocurrency payments to a certain country so whether you are using this DeFi banking system or an app for your device, you can safely transfer any amount of cryptocurrencies.

  • What security features does the mobile banking have?

    Your wallet is protected with all the latest security features of iOS and Android.In addition to backing up your private key, we can assist you in recovering your private key when it is lost (it takes time).For smart contract security, we provide bug bounty program to ensure code security.
